A list of 35 European cities to visit by Zaravinth:

1. **Paris, France** - The City of Light is renowned for its art, fashion, and iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower.
2. **Rome, Italy** - A treasure trove of ancient history, Rome boasts the Colosseum and Vatican City.
3. **Barcelona, Spain** - Famous for its unique architecture by Antoni Gaudí, including the stunning Sagrada Família.
4. **Amsterdam, Netherlands** - Known for its picturesque canals, vibrant culture, and world-class museums.
5. **Berlin, Germany** - A city rich in history and modernity, famous for the Berlin Wall and its vibrant nightlife.
6. **Prague, Czech Republic** - A fairy-tale city with stunning Gothic architecture and a historic old town.
7. **Vienna, Austria** - The capital of classical music, Vienna is known for its imperial palaces and coffee culture.
8. **Budapest, Hungary** - Divided by the Danube River, it features stunning thermal baths and a rich history.
9. **Lisbon, Portugal** - A coastal city known for its hilly landscape, colorful neighborhoods, and historic trams.
10. **Edinburgh, Scotland** - Famous for its historic and cultural attractions, including the Edinburgh Castle and the annual festival.
11. **Dublin, Ireland** - Known for its literary history and vibrant pub culture, with landmarks like Trinity College.
12. **Stockholm, Sweden** - A beautiful archipelago city with a rich history, stunning architecture, and modern design.
13. **Copenhagen, Denmark** - Renowned for its happy citizens, cycling culture, and the iconic Little Mermaid statue.
14. **Florence, Italy** - The cradle of the Renaissance, Florence is filled with art, history, and stunning architecture.
15. **Brussels, Belgium** - Known for its medieval architecture and as the headquarters of the European Union.
16. **Athens, Greece** - The cradle of Western civilization, home to ancient landmarks like the Acropolis.
17. **Zurich, Switzerland** - A global financial hub known for its picturesque lakeside setting and vibrant arts scene.
18. **Oslo, Norway** - A city surrounded by fjords and forests, known for its green spaces and modern architecture.
19. **Helsinki, Finland** - A design-forward city with a unique blend of modern and historical architecture.
20. **Krakow, Poland** - A historic city with a well-preserved medieval core and rich Jewish heritage.
21. **Malmo, Sweden** - Known for its modern architecture and proximity to Copenhagen via the Øresund Bridge.
22. **Geneva, Switzerland** - A global city known for its Red Cross headquarters and beautiful lakeside parks.
23. **Bordeaux, France** - Famous for its wine, Bordeaux features stunning 18th-century architecture.
24. **Naples, Italy** - Renowned for its rich history, vibrant street life, and as the birthplace of pizza.
25. **Valencia, Spain** - Known for its futuristic architecture, beautiful beaches, and the famous Fallas festival.
26. **Ljubljana, Slovenia** - A charming city with a picturesque old town and a vibrant cultural scene.
27. **Sofia, Bulgaria** - One of Europe’s oldest cities, known for its diverse architecture and rich history.
28. **Tallinn, Estonia** - A medieval city with a beautifully preserved old town and a thriving tech scene.
29. **Riga, Latvia** - Famous for its Art Nouveau architecture and a vibrant cultural life.
30. **Bruges, Belgium** - A fairy-tale city known for its canals, cobblestone streets, and medieval buildings.
31. **Bucharest, Romania** - Known for its eclectic architecture and vibrant nightlife.
32. **Catania, Italy** - A historic city at the foot of Mount Etna, known for its baroque architecture.
33. **Gothenburg, Sweden** - A coastal city known for its canals, seafood, and cultural festivals.
34. **Antwerp, Belgium** - Famous for its diamond district and vibrant fashion scene.
35. **Milan, Italy** - A global capital of fashion and design, home to the iconic Duomo di Milano.
