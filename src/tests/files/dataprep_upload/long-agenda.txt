
Child's Basketball Camp - 2-Day Event Plan

Day 1:
-------
08:00 AM - 09:00 AM: Registration and Welcome
- Parents and children arrive at the camp.
- Registration desk setup for check-in.
- Welcome speech by the camp director.
- Distribution of camp T-shirts and water bottles.

09:00 AM - 10:30 AM: Warm-Up and Stretching
- Light jogging around the court.
- Dynamic stretching exercises.
- Introduction to basic basketball drills.

10:30 AM - 12:00 PM: Skill Development Session 1
- Dribbling drills: Cone dribbling, figure-eight dribbling.
- Passing drills: Chest pass, bounce pass, overhead pass.
- Shooting drills: Layups, free throws, jump shots.

12:00 PM - 01:00 PM: Lunch Break
- Healthy lunch provided by the camp.
- Hydration and rest.

01:00 PM - 02:30 PM: Team Building Activities
- Icebreaker games to build camaraderie.
- Trust exercises and team challenges.
- Relay races and fun competitions.

02:30 PM - 04:00 PM: Skill Development Session 2
- Defensive drills: Stance, footwork, and positioning.
- Rebounding drills: Box out techniques, grabbing rebounds.
- Offensive drills: Cutting, screening, and spacing.

04:00 PM - 05:30 PM: Scrimmage Games
- Dividing children into teams.
- Friendly matches to apply learned skills.
- Coaches providing feedback and guidance.

05:30 PM - 06:00 PM: Cool Down and Stretching
- Light jogging and walking.
- Static stretching exercises.
- Reflection on the day's activities.

06:00 PM - 07:00 PM: Dinner Break
- Nutritious dinner provided by the camp.
- Socializing and relaxation.

07:00 PM - 08:30 PM: Evening Entertainment
- Movie night with a basketball-themed film.
- Popcorn and snacks.
- Fun games and activities.

08:30 PM - 09:00 PM: Wind Down and Bedtime
- Quiet time for reading or journaling.
- Lights out and rest for the next day.

Day 2:
-------
08:00 AM - 09:00 AM: Morning Warm-Up and Stretching
- Light jogging around the court.
- Dynamic stretching exercises.
- Review of previous day's skills.

09:00 AM - 10:30 AM: Skill Development Session 3
- Advanced dribbling drills: Crossovers, behind-the-back dribbling.
- Advanced passing drills: No-look pass, wrap-around pass.
- Advanced shooting drills: Three-pointers, fadeaways.

10:30 AM - 12:00 PM: Mini Tournaments
- Dividing children into teams.
- Round-robin style mini tournaments.
- Prizes for winning teams.

12:00 PM - 01:00 PM: Lunch Break
- Healthy lunch provided by the camp.
- Hydration and rest.

01:00 PM - 02:30 PM: Guest Speaker Session
- Talk by a professional basketball player or coach.
- Q&A session with the guest speaker.
- Autograph and photo opportunities.

02:30 PM - 04:00 PM: Fun and Games
- Basketball-themed obstacle course.
- Shooting contests: Free throw contest, three-point contest.
- Dribbling contests: Timed dribbling challenges.

04:00 PM - 05:30 PM: Final Scrimmage Games
- Dividing children into teams.
- Friendly matches to showcase skills learned.
- Coaches providing feedback and awards.

05:30 PM - 06:00 PM: Cool Down and Stretching
- Light jogging and walking.
- Static stretching exercises.
- Reflection on the day's activities.

06:00 PM - 07:00 PM: Dinner Break
- Nutritious dinner provided by the camp.
- Socializing and relaxation.

07:00 PM - 08:30 PM: Awards Ceremony and Farewell
- Presentation of certificates and awards.
- Closing speech by the camp director.
- Group photos and goodbyes.

08:30 PM - 09:00 PM: Departure
- Parents arrive to pick up their children.
- Distribution of camp memorabilia.
- Final goodbyes and departure.
